Kheer u shall lick the plate clean!!

are u ready to learn how to make it?

The ingiedients

  • Milk: 1 liter (full-fat or whole milk works best)
  • Rice: 1/4 cup (usually Basmati, washed and soaked for 30 minutes)
  • Sugar: 1/4 to 1/3 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom Powder: 1/4 teaspoon

The steps

  1. Step 1: Cook the Rice Wash and soak 1/4 cup of rice for 30 minutes. In a heavy-bottomed pan, boil 1 liter of milk. Once boiling, lower the heat and add the soaked rice. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  2. Step 2: Add Sweetness and Flavor Once the rice is cooked and the milk has reduced slightly (about 15-20 minutes), add 1/4 to 1/3 cup of sugar and stir well. Add 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder for flavor.
  3. Step 3: Add Dry Fruits Optional: Roast chopped almonds, cashews, and raisins in 1 tablespoon of ghee until golden. Mix the roasted dry fruits and saffron strands (soaked in warm milk) into the kheer. Let it simmer for another 5-10 minutes until creamy. Serve warm or chilled, and enjoy!
